Privacy Policy

Welcome to the NTU Apprentice, a website operated by Nottingham Trent University. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy is intended to inform you about how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal data when you visit our website at https://ntuapprenticeportaldev.ntu.ac.uk/.

By using our website, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. Please take a moment to read this policy and ensure you understand its content. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us using the information provided below.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you visit our website:

Personal Information

  • Name
  • Contact information (email address, phone number, address)
  • Date of birth
  • Username and password
  • Other information you provide to us

Automatically Collected Information

We may also collect information about your device and how you use our website, including:

  • Your IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages you visit
  • Date and time of your visit
  • Referring and exit pages

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Providing Services

  • To provide you with access to the NTU Apprentice Portal and its features.
  • To process and manage your account.
  • To communicate with you regarding your account and our services.

Improving Our Website

  • To analyse how users interact with our website.
  • To enhance and personalize your user experience.
  • To fix technical issues and optimize our site’s performance.

Communication

  • To send you updates, newsletters, and other relevant information.
  • To respond to your inquiries and support requests.
  • To inform you about changes to our services or policies.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information under the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist us in operating our website and providing services.
  • When required by law or to protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property.
  • In the event of a business transfer, such as a merger, acquisition, or sale.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect data about your usage of our website. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Please refer to our Cookie Policy for more details.

Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and employ reasonable safeguards to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Your Choices

You have the right to:

  • Review and update your personal information.
  • Opt-out of receiving promotional emails.
  • Request the deletion of your account and associated data.

Children’s Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with their information, please contact us.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the “Last Updated” date will be revised accordingly.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data handling practices, please contact us here

We will respond to your inquiries promptly.

By using the NTU Apprentice Portal, you agree to the terms and practices described in this Privacy Policy.
